# Video Standards (Every Video)

* Start with a real-world problem.
* Explain the intuition before introducing terminology.
* Use clean visual diagrams and animations throughout.
* Include complexity, trade-offs, and common mistakes.
* Show production examples from major tech companies where appropriate.
* End with a concise recap and recommended next topics.
* Keep reusable Notion notes and downloadable diagrams aligned with the video.
