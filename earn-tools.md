## Monetization strategy

Use ads carefully:

| Placement                                 | Use?                |
| ----------------------------------------- | ------------------- |
| Above the tool before user can act        | Avoid               |
| After intro before tool                   | Maybe, one small ad |
| After result/download section             | Good                |
| Middle of article content                 | Good                |
| Between upload button and download button | Avoid               |
| Near related tools                        | Good                |

## What to avoid

Avoid making these your main AdSense strategy:

```txt
YouTube downloader
TikTok downloader
Instagram downloader
Credit card generator
Fake chat generator
Fake bill/salary/certificate tools
AI news spam
Generic quotes/status pages
```

Google’s AdSense policy guidance warns against ads around copyrighted content or links that enable unauthorized copyrighted material, so video downloader pages are risky for long-term monetization. ([Medium][9]) Google also says helpful, reliable, people-first content is what ranking systems aim to reward. ([Google for Developers][10])
